# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

**Q: What devices support TDLiDAR?**
A: TDLiDAR requires an iPhone 12 Pro or newer with a built-in LiDAR sensor.

**Q: How accurate are the scans?**
A: TDLiDAR provides millimeter-level accuracy for most indoor environments.

**Q: Can I export my scans?**
A: Yes, scans can be exported in multiple formats including PLY, OBJ, and USDZ.

## Troubleshooting

**Q: Why are my scans blurry?**
A: Ensure you're moving slowly and smoothly. Avoid sudden movements or lighting changes.

**Q: The app crashes during scanning**
A: Try clearing the app cache or reinstalling. Ensure you have sufficient storage.
